Like the other collision detections we must first get the positions of the object from the canvas.coords() method. Then, we will detect if the bottom of the Ball has hit the top of the Paddle. However, we must also be sure that the left side of the Ball is further RIGHT than the left side of the Paddle, and that the right side of the Ball is further Left than the LEFT side of the Paddle.
